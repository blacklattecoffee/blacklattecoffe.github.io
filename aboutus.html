About us

Founded in 2014, "Hendel" is already recognized as one of the leaders in development and production of cosmetics and biologically active food supplements. At the present time "Hendel" is a dynamically developing company consisting of true professionals.

Our vision - we would like to become a Company that will be ahead of current trends and fully manage the needs of the most demanding customers.

Our mission – we create health and beauty products for ambitious and persistent people, try to make the lives of our customers better and richer by creating safe and natural products of high quality.

Our main goal is creation and market launch of a wide range of products that are unique in their market segment and able to fully meet the expectations of our customers.

Our advantages:

  1. We use natural and completely safe ingredients
  2. We develop unique product formulas
  3. Our ingredients come only from reliable and trusted suppliers
  4. We control the entire cycle of production and release of the products according to international and Russian standards (Technical Regulation of Customs Union, ISO, GMPC)
